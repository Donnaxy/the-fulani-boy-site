About the Author

Nasridden is a storyteller whose roots and journey shape his words. Through Twist of Fate: Ada Eze and the Fulani Boy, he weaves together themes of love, culture, and identity to create a narrative that resonates with readers worldwide.

Writing from lived experiences and imagination, Nasridden brings heart and authenticity to every chapter, exploring the beauty and complexity of human connection across boundaries.
